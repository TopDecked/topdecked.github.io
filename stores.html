ARE YOU A STORE OR EVENT ORGANIZER?

We have something "in store" for you, too. Easily and cost-effectively promote your events - build happy, healthy, growing community of players.

WE BELIEVE IN MAGIC

By using the best of mobile technology, social media, and behavioral research, we're working to bring players into your store, help keep you connected with them, and build a healthier community --- one where you spend less time on Facebook, and more time on business.

  • PROMOTE YOUR EVENTS, automatically

    Tired of spending hours on Facebook? Just schedule events like normal, and we will import them into our system for you. Players in your area will be notified of a new event that meets their interests.

    Any additional information you choose to post to Facebook is automatically included in your event listings, and if there's something you want to change, we give you total control of the details.

  • GET MORE PLAYERS

    Pre-registration and careful promotion has been shown to increase event turnout. Add your payment information details and turn on pre-registration - we'll take care of getting the players to your store.

    We'll also help you understand your players' behaviors, so you can create events and promotions that keep them coming back.

  • Smart SCHEDULING

    Not sure of the best day or time to hold your event? Don't wonder any longer. Our built-in schedule helper will suggest dates for highest player turnout and lowest nearby competition. Never lose out to another big regional tournament again.

  • SPEND MORE TIME RUNNING YOUR STORE

    Busy on game-day? Spend less time at the cash register and more time running your business. Players can pay on-site - we'll make sure all the paperwork is handled.

  • BUILT IN ANALYTICS

    Not sure what your players like to do most? We'll keep track of your best performing events, days of the week, leagues, and more. Just encourage players to use our app, and we'll give you news ideas and new ways to keep them engaged.
